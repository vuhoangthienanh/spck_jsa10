Our store

At our store, we're serving up many different kinds of foods. In fact, our special recipe is one of our unexpected specialties. Reminiscent of butcher shops back in the day, each slow-smoked, sizzling prime chop measures seven-fingers high. Our signature recipe, that we have perfected for more than four years, is rubbed with a secret blend of seasonings, cured and roasted on a rotisserie with pecan wood for up to six hours before it's topped with our store signature herb-garlic butter, then carved tableside.
